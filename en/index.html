Who we are




ARAM is mainly dedicated to supply prime raw materials & structured financial solutions to the Brazilian agribusiness.


Young company, experienced people: ARAM is a startup led by executives bearing 30+ years of experience with the base business and the financial market, including global senior management positions with Dow, Lyondell, Bayer, Safra Bank, JBS.

Successful Track Record: ​​ARAM is a continuation of other business structures that have pioneered in importing & distributing base raw materials into Brazil since January 2013, from China, South Korea, France, Spain, Germany, US, Middle East.

Financial backup: ARAM has got backup by investors from the financial industry / investment banking with interests in the manufacturing industry, mining and agribusiness in Brazil and abroad, that have been supporting its continuous development, growth and financial capability.

Knowledge: ARAM has the expertise to deal with the complex tax and legal Brazilian business environment and has also got all the mandatory licenses required to move agribusiness supplies.

ESG: ARAM is fully committed to upholding environmental, social and governance (ESG) compliance, either intercompany as within its whole business environment.

Mission

Generate VALUE for clients, shareholders and society through the distribution of materials in a sustainable way.


Vision

Build and develop an Agrofintech company focused in trading & distributing agribusiness supplies and products, jointly delivering structured financial solutions, and thru this unique combination, become within the next five years one of the top 30 players in the Brazilian Agribusiness.
